Indoor Plants & Seeds

Fruit Seeds & Plants | Veg Seeds & Plants | Outdoor Seeds & Plants

Aloe

  • • Sow indoors
  • • Warm kitchen windowsill
  • • Place in a warm position
  • • Germination may be slow

Cactus

  • • Thrives in bright light
  • • Feed once a month
  • • Light watering when dry
  • • Easy to look after

Snake Plant

  • • Most light conditions
  • • Keep dust free
  • • Light watering
  • • Stores oxygen daily

Calathea

  • • Regular watering
  • • They love hot climates
  • • Light but no direct sun
  • • Keep clean of dust
