Fruit Seeds & Plants

Currant Plants

  • • One bush yields 20lbs
  • • Grows well in sun and shade
  • • Sweet fruit
  • • Highly nutritious

Raspberry Plant

  • • A sunny, sheltered position is ideal
  • • Usually grown in upright rows
  • • Requires post and wire support
  • • Free of perennial weeds as possible.

Blueberry Plants

  • • Great for container growing
  • • Varieties will reach to 1.8m (6ft)
  • • Delicious nutritious summer fruits
  • • Bigger pots mean healthier roots

Strawberry Seeds

  • • Sow indoors December to March
  • • Maintain 20°C (68°F).
  • • A warm kitchen windowsill is fine
  • • Plant into individual containers
