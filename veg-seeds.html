Brocolli

  • • Excellent source of vitamin C
  • • High in antioxidants
  • • Solid, domed heads
  • • Medium sized beads

Carrots

  • • A light, well-drained soil is right
  • • Does not thrive on heavy clay
  • • Sow outdoors thinly where they are to crop
  • • Seedlings usually appear in 14-21 days.

Beetroot

  • • The roots become pink when cooked.
  • • Perfect for adding interest to salads
  • • Attractive red and white rings
  • • A mild flavour

Peas

  • • Sow outdoors
  • • Seedlings in 10-18 days approx.
  • • Early sowings benefit from frost
  • • Pick regularly for the best yields.
